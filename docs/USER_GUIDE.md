## ğŸ“ Managing Tasks

### Creating a Task
1. Click the **"New Task"** button in the top right corner of the Dashboard.
2. Fill in the task details:
   - **Title**: What needs to be done?
   - **Description**: Add more context (optional).
   - **Status**: Todo, In Progress, or Done.
   - **Priority**: Low, Medium, High, or Urgent.
   - **Category**: Select a category (or leave as "No Category").
   - **Due Date**: When is it due?
3. Click **"Create Task"**.

### Editing a Task
- Click the **Edit** (pencil) icon on any task card.
- Update the details and click **"Save Changes"**.

### Deleting a Task
- Click the **Delete** (trash can) icon on any task card.
- Confirm the deletion. **Note:** This action cannot be undone.

## ğŸ“‚ Categories

Organize your tasks by creating custom categories.

1. Click **"Manage Categories"** in the Dashboard header.
2. **Create**: Type a name (e.g., "Work", "Personal") and press Enter or click the Plus icon.
3. **Delete**: Click the Trash icon next to a category to remove it.
   - *Note: Deleting a category does not delete the tasks within it; they just become uncategorized.*

---

## ğŸ” Filtering & Sorting

Use the controls at the top of the task list to find what you need.

- **Search**: Type in the search bar to find tasks by title or description.
- **Status Filter**: Show only "Todo", "In Progress", or "Done" tasks.
- **Priority Filter**: Show tasks by urgency level.
- **Category Filter**: Show tasks belonging to a specific category.
- **Sort By**: Order tasks by Date Created, Priority, or Due Date.
